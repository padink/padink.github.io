WELCOME TO DANTALIYA DENTISTRY

Dantalaya or “Danta-chikitsalaya” is a platform exclusively for practicing dental surgeons in India. It promises to produce smiles for the entire population of the country by bringing together dental surgeons across India.

For the first time in India, dental practitioners all over the country will come together to provide dental treatment and counselling for every individual of the country. The treatment is not restricted to Indian citizens alone but people from any nationality can avail Dental treatment and advice while in the country.

Dantalaya aims to take dental health services in the country to a all-new level by making Dental treatment procedures affordable all over the country. Rural and urban practitioners will practice dentistry in the same level.

Dantalaya has something for everyone: For oral-health friendly Treatments, To Find a Dentist in Nearby Location, Treatment at Affordable price With EMI options, Review oral health history . Dantalaya now to brush up on your oral health and prepared to meet Dental Practitioners from various specialties with lots of experience in clinical practice!
